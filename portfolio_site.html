First author · LoResLM Workshop, EACL 2026

Domain-Specific Quality Estimation for Machine Translation in Low-Resource Scenarios

Built a two-track evaluation system for English→Indic machine translation across Healthcare, Legal, Tourism, and General content. Benchmarked closed-weight (Gemini) and open-weight (Llama, Qwen, Gemma) models under zero-shot, few-shot, and guideline-anchored prompting — then designed a lightweight adapter (LoRA/LoRMA + intermediate-layer regression head, following the ALOPE framework) that rescued the domain/language pairs where prompting alone fell short.

65,200sentence pairs, human-scored (train+val+test)
4domains: Healthcare, Legal, Tourism, General
